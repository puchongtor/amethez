## INTUITING COLOUR USING A PENDULUM (p.124)
- A clear crystal pendulum is preferable; a clear glass bead or button on chain also works
- Hold the chain so crystal hangs down; place crystal over outstretched palm of the other hand; ask the pendulum a question
- Swings clockwise = "yes"; swings to the left = "no"; swings backwards and forwards = no conclusion
- You can use crystals or buttons of different colours for different questions
- Example: for affairs of the heart, use turquoise; for business and finance, use green crystal

### The Colour Wheel
- Draw a large circle of white paper; divide into pie-piece wedges
- In each section, put a basic colour with its tint and shade (e.g., red, pink and maroon)
- Hold pendulum in centre of wheel; ask a question; allow the pendulum to move to whichever section the board wants to go to
- The colour section it swings towards gives psychic colour clues to the information you desire
- Refer to the psychological profile of your chosen colour to analyse the information
- Always remember that any colour intuiting process can only give indications; the art is in the interpretation
