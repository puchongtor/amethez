## Understanding Crystals

- Crystals: naturally occurring solids made from minerals
- Most formed in the earth's surface via inorganic processes (sedimentation, etc.)
- Some, like moldavite, are born in the stars — arrive as meteors
- Scientific definition: solid form of a mineral or substance with precise atomic arrangement and mineral composition — unique
- Identified by: crystal form, color, specific gravity, hardness (Mohs Scale)
- Mohs Scale: named after Friedrich Mohs (German inventor), measures relative hardness of minerals

### 7 Natural Crystal Systems
1. Cubic
2. Hexagonal
3. Tetragonal
4. Rhombohedral
5. Orthorhombic
6. Monoclinic
7. Triclinic

- Cubic crystal (e.g., aspyrite): always has symmetry of a cube
- Hexagonal crystal (e.g., quartz): always has six sides

### Crystal Shapes (Polished)
- Point/obelisk
- Sphere
- Egg
- Heart
- Angel
- Faceted stone
- Tumble stone (most widely available — tumbled in barrels with grits to smooth and polish)

### A Note on Terminology
- "Crystal" in this book = not limited to scientific definition — includes all mineral kingdom: crystals, minerals, rocks, stones, gemstones — also called "Stone People"

7. **Crystalline energies**: place on an amethyst bed, quartz cluster, or inside a geode — crystalline energies cleanse and clear; a large amethyst bed can be like a vacation for your crystals
8. **Sounds**: chanting, drumming, or playing Tibetan bells or cymbals can clear unhelpful vibrations
9. **Sunlight**: cleanse by leaving in sunlight; also dries crystals after washing. Caution: quartz crystals, especially crystal balls, can focus sun's rays and be a fire risk — take appropriate precautions
10. **Breath, light, or Reiki**: breathe over the crystal to cleanse it; Reiki can be practiced on crystals; meditation technique using light visualization — still your mind, picture a bright light flowing from your brow chakra to the crystal in your hand, washing over it, filling the crystal — when it completely glows with light, slowly bring focus away from crystal back to surroundings

## The Aura: The Body's Energy Field

- Your aura = the etheric energy field surrounding you
- Should not be thought of as separate — it is your energy system, as much a part of you as your hands, head, and hormones
- Energy flows from your body through your chakras into your aura and beyond to the outside world; at the same time also flows in reverse direction: from outside world into you
- The aura is the first line of defense of the body's energy system — like the skin is the first line of defense of the immune system
- When your aura is strong and healthy: you are less likely to become ill; more likely to attract friends, develop relationships, experience a happy life
- The aura plays several important roles in crystal chakra healing: connects you to your environment and all external factors — people and events, as well as pollutants that affect body, mind, spirit, and emotions

### The 7 Layers of the Aura

1. **Physical aura** — working outward from skin; very narrow, extending no more than ⅓ in/1cm from skin; very difficult to separate from the sensation you feel when a slight breeze ruffles the hairs on your arm; your energy communication channel; connection to all energies surrounding you

2. **Etheric aura** — second layer; one of the easiest to sense with pendulum or hand; starts about ⅓ in/1cm out from body; about ¾–1½ in/2–4cm thick; first part of aura people see when beginning to see auras — always appears with a slight gray or purple tint; protective barrier to the physical body — defends against dis-ease held in outer areas of aura

3. **Emotional aura** — extending a further 1½–2¼ in/4–6cm; this is the layer in your energy field where you hold emotion; here, like attracts like — over time, good feelings group together, as do bad ones; bad emotions grow as dark bits of energy, grouping and exerting a "pull" on other dark energy, slowly growing into larger masses that draw energy from wherever they can; these dark blobs in the emotional aura create a feeling of draining around a person — the sense that someone is draining you or you don't feel comfortable around them

4. **Vital aura** — essential to health; dis-ease appears in this part of the aura well before any physical symptoms in the body; everything that has happened to you in your life is recorded here; psychics who specialize in "aura readings" look here to see past, present, and future; unlike most other layers, the vital aura changes size and color continuously, according to your health, moods, feelings, and emotions — with practice you can expand and contract it at will

5. **Astral aura** — another layer that changes size and shape dramatically; involved in out-of-body experiences, astral travel, and projection; acts as a bridge between you and the rest of the universe — it is where you start to sense your surroundings

6. **Lower mental plane** — can be imagined as an elliptical membrane surrounding each person; less than 1mm thick; the edge of the aura and boundary of your energy system; occasionally holes develop in the lower mental plane, which can lead to an oozing of energy, making you lethargic and prone to minor illness; these holes can be healed by "combing" a quartz crystal through the aura (see page 137)

7. **Higher mental plane** — there is much debate about this final layer; appears to hover about the head, about 1⅓ in/4cm above the lower mental plane; many people think it is connected to the lower mental plane, but it is the connection to spirit, God, or universal energy; the higher mental plane is the layer most susceptible to influence from things other people say; the more you use your ability to contact the spirit world, the easier it becomes; when this has become normal for you, you will see the guiding hand of the universe all around you
