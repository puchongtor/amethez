## p.10–21 — PRINCIPLES OF CRYSTAL HEALING

### p.12–13 — How Crystals Form
- Crystals form through three processes: igneous (cooling magma), sedimentary (pressure/heat over time), metamorphic (existing rock transformed by heat/pressure)
- Crystal structure = atoms arranged in repeating geometric patterns; this structure determines properties
- Crystal lattice vibrates at measurable frequencies
- Piezoelectric effect: quartz generates electric charge under pressure (used in watches, electronics)

### p.22–23 — Exploring Crystals
- Hold crystal in both hands, close eyes, breathe deeply
- Notice physical sensations: tingling, warmth, coolness, pulsing
- Notice emotional responses: calm, energy, sadness, joy
- Each person experiences crystals differently; personal response is valid

### p.24–25 — Meditation with Crystals
- Hold crystal or place on relevant chakra
- Focus on breath; allow crystal's energy to merge with yours
- 10–20 minutes; journal afterwards

### p.26–27 — Hand-held Crystal Healing
- Hold crystal in non-dominant hand for receiving
- Hold in dominant hand for directing/projecting energy
- Scan body with crystal to find areas of imbalance (tingling, resistance, heat)

### p.28–29 — Crystal Wands
- Natural quartz point or faceted wand
- Use to draw energy patterns, trace meridians, clear blockages
- Clockwise circles = draw energy in; counterclockwise = release energy

## p.54–67 — DIRECTORY OF CRYSTALS

### RED STONES (p.55) — Chakra: Base/Root — Energize, Activate, Protect
- **GARNET:** Speediest energizer; activates other stones placed nearby; increases energy wherever placed; varieties include Almandine, Pyrope, Spessartine
- **RUBY:** Red variety of Corundum; works with heart centre and circulation; energizing but balancing; expensive — good quality needed for healing
- **JASPER (Red/Dark):** Dark or brick-red massive quartz; found on beaches; grounding, gently activating; place near Base chakra

### PINK STONES (p.56) — Chakra: Base + Heart — Gentle resolution, emotions, self-worth
- **ROSE QUARTZ:** Best known pink stone; calming and reassuring; powerful releaser of unexpressed emotions; master stone of the heart
- **RHODONITE:** Salmon pink flecked with black when polished; grounding effect greater than other pink stones; works with practical aspects of feelings
- **KUNZITE:** Pale pink/violet striated crystal; excellent shifter of emotional debris; helps self-expression
- **RHODOCROSITE:** Delicate banded stone — pink, yellow, orange; improves self-image and self-worth; balances 2nd and 3rd chakras

### ORANGE STONES (p.57) — Chakra: Sacral (2nd) — Creativity, flow, assimilation
- **CARNELIAN:** Most popular orange stone; warmth, gently energizing; found on beaches; nearly always beneficial
- **ORANGE CALCITE:** Smooth, lustrous; ideal for encouragement of potential; softness/watery feel good at melting away problems
- **DARK CITRINE (orange-brown):** Balanced stimulator for practical creative skills; gentle grounding and supporting
- **TOPAZ:** Elongated crystal with parallel striations; excellent clearing stone to direct energy around the body
- **COPPER (Native):** Found as natural nuggets and crystallized forms; ideal when lack of flow creates stagnation; beneficial effect on many physical and subtle systems

### YELLOW STONES (p.58) — Chakra: Solar Plexus (3rd) — Mind, nervous system, digestion
- **AMBER:** Fossil tree resin; lemon yellow to rich orange-brown (also deep red and green); beneficial on nervous system and self-healing processes
- **RUTILATED QUARTZ:** Clear/smoky quartz with fine threads of golden/orange rutile; excellent for moving healing energy; works well with broken/damaged tissues
- **TIGER'S EYE:** Quartz with velvety sheen, bands of browns/golds/reds; speeds up energy flow; anchors subtle changes into physical body
- **CITRINE QUARTZ:** Popular yellow stone; bright clear yellow = keeps mind clear and focused
- **IRON PYRITES:** "Fool's gold"; cleanses and strengthens; calms the digestive system

### GREEN STONES (p.59) — Chakra: Heart (4th) — Balance, relationships, growth, space
- **GREEN AVENTURINE:** Excellent heart balancer; gently expansive; easy expression of feelings
- **MALACHITE:** Digs out deep feelings, hurts and resentments; breaks unwanted ties and patterns of behaviour
- **BLOODSTONE:** Green quartz with flecks of red jasper; active balance of energy and calm; stimulates emotional growth; benefits heart and circulation
- **AMAZONITE:** Calms and balances emotions; helps throat and lung problems
- **MOSS AGATE:** Ideal for supporting lungs; eases breathing difficulties; eases feelings of emotional stifling; brings in energy of natural world
- **PERIDOT:** Vivid light green; associated with volcanic activity; one of best cleansers of subtle bodies; motivates growth and necessary change
- **EMERALD:** Green variety of mineral Beryl; helps finding personal direction in activity; brings clarity and calm to heart and emotions
- **BERYL (Green):** Also shown — heart/growth energy

### LIGHT BLUE STONES (p.60) — Chakra: Throat (5th) — Communication, expression, all senses
- **AQUAMARINE:** Blue variety of Beryl; stimulates healing properties of body; improves confidence and ability to stand ground; releases flow of communication
- **TURQUOISE:** Used for centuries as supportive and protective stone; strengthens all subtle bodies and fine communication systems (meridians)
- **BLUE LACE AGATE:** Beautiful banded variety of quartz; gently cools and calms; lightens thought; works well with Rose Quartz
- **CELESTITE:** Soft stone forming clear, delicate blue crystals; inspiring and "dreamy"; lifts heavy moods; helps expressing spiritual thoughts; helpful for throat problems

### INDIGO STONES (p.61) — Chakra: Brow/Third Eye (6th) — Intuition, perception, understanding
- **LAPIS LAZULI:** Rock of different minerals — large proportion of deep blue Lazurite + iron pyrites; powerful effect; stimulates rapid release of stresses; enables greater peace; stimulates higher faculties of mind and understanding
- **SODALITE:** Similar to Lapis but less vivid blue with cloudy white veils; calms mind; allows new information to be received
- **KYANITE:** Translucent thin blades, often look like fans; wonderful mover of blocked/inflamed energy; calming, clearing, rapid restorer of equilibrium
- **AZURITE:** Commonly seen as small round nodules or shiny crystals; frees up difficult long-standing blocks in communication; reveals structures stopping us from using full potential; stimulates memory and recall
- **SAPPHIRE:** Can be expensive when deep enough colour; relaxes and improves mind; balances all aspects of self by releasing tension and promoting peace of mind

### VIOLET STONES (p.62) — Chakra: Crown (7th) — Inspiration, imagination, empathy, service
- **AMETHYST:** Perhaps most useful all-purpose healing crystal; universally applicable; good for meditation — quietens mind; allows finer perceptions to become clear
- **FLUORITE:** Wide variety of colours, violet most common; used with upper chakras; integrates subtle energies with normal consciousness; helps coordination — physical and mental
- **SUGILITE:** Various shades of purple; useful in group situations — resolves personal difficulties, brings group coherence; for those who feel they don't "fit in"
- **IOLITE:** Also called water sapphire; subtle violet translucence; increases imagination and all aspects of intuitive creativity

### WHITE STONES (p.63) — Chakra: Crown — Clarity, purification, universal energy
- **CLEAR QUARTZ:** Strengthens and brings coherent energy; amplifies all subtle systems; brings state of harmony; Master healer
- **MILKY QUARTZ:** Same as Clear but gentler effect; all subtle systems enhanced and clarified
- **HERKIMER DIAMOND:** Clear, bright variety of quartz; excellent for all detoxification processes; cleanses imbalances from subtle bodies; amplifies qualities of nearby stones; induces clarity of mind and vivid dream recall
- **ICELAND SPA (Optical Calcite):** Variety of clear calcite; helps relate to world in balanced and productive way; brings clarity
- **SELENITE:** Named for soft moonlight quality; type of gypsum; DO NOT place in damp/wet — thin slices slide apart and disintegrate; good for removing emotional turmoil/confusion; clarifies awareness; helps reach new states of consciousness
