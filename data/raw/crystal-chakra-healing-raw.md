## p.9–16 — CH.1: INTRODUCING CRYSTAL CHAKRA HEALING

### History & Origins (p.10–11)
- Taoist healing: energy (chi) flows through meridians
- Ayurvedic medicine (India): same concept, called prana; chakras described in ancient Sanskrit texts
- Theophrastus *Peri Lithon* (315 BCE): first Western text on crystals
- California Institute for Human Science: modern scientific study of energy fields
- Institute of Life Physics, Tokyo: research on subtle energy
- Dr Hiroshi Motoyama: chakra research using scientific measurement

### Crystal Systems (p.12–13)
- All crystals formed in one of 7 systems: Cubic, Hexagonal, Trigonal, Tetragonal, Orthorhombic, Monoclinic, Triclinic
- Crystal system determines energy pattern and healing application
- Cubic: isometric — 3 equal axes at 90° (Garnet, Diamond, Pyrite, Fluorite)
- Hexagonal: 4 axes, 3 horizontal at 120° (Emerald, Aquamarine, Beryl)
- Trigonal: like hexagonal but different symmetry (Quartz, Calcite, Ruby, Sapphire)
- Orthorhombic: 3 unequal axes at 90° (Peridot, Topaz, Aragonite)
- Monoclinic: 3 unequal axes, 2 at right angles (Malachite, Jade, Moonstone)
- Triclinic: 3 unequal axes, none at right angles (Labradorite, Turquoise, Kyanite)

### Crystalline Energies (p.14–15)
- Piezoelectric effect: quartz generates electric charge under pressure
- Pyroelectric effect: crystals generate charge from temperature change
- Record keeper crystals: raised triangles on face = access to akashic records
- Crystals-as-genes theory: crystalline structure stores information like DNA

### Understanding Crystals (p.16–17)
- Each crystal has unique atomic structure = unique energy pattern
- Color = key indicator of chakra association
- Size doesn't determine strength — intention and connection more important
- Trust intuition when selecting: the right crystal often "jumps out"

## p.18–37 — CH.2: WORKING WITH CRYSTALS

### Selecting Crystals (p.18–19)
- Let crystal choose you — notice which draws your eye or hand
- Pendulum: hold over crystal, clockwise = yes/good match, counterclockwise = no
- Touch: hold crystal, notice sensations (tingling, warmth, pulsing)
- Hemimorphite good for working with angels/spirit guides

### Natural Crystal Shapes (p.20–21)
- **Natural point:** directs energy in one direction
- **Double terminated:** works both ways; balancing; bridges chakras
- **Cluster:** radiates in all directions; ideal for rooms/groups
- **Tumbled:** gentle, general healing; good for beginners
- **Geode:** holds/amplifies; protective
- **Sphere:** omnidirectional; meditation/scrying
- **Egg:** concentrated energy; fertility; new beginnings
- **Pyramid:** focuses energy at apex; manifestation

### Cleansing Crystals (p.22–25)
- Running water: hold under cold running water 1–2 min (NOT for water-soluble stones)
- Sunlight: leave in sunlight 1–4 hours (NOT for color-fading stones like Amethyst)
- Moonlight: full moon overnight; gentle, effective for all
- Earth: bury in earth overnight (most thorough)
- Smudging: pass through sage/incense smoke
- Sound: Tibetan singing bowl, bells
- Other crystals: place on/near Carnelian, Kyanite, or Citrine (self-cleansing)
- Intent: hold crystal, visualize white/golden light purifying it
- WARNING: Some crystals damage in water — check before cleansing with water

### Elixirs / Gem Remedies (p.28–29)
- Indirect method (safe for all): place crystal in sealed glass container in water
- Direct method: crystal placed directly in water (only for non-toxic, water-safe crystals)
- Leave in sunlight 2–4 hours
- Drink small amount or use topically
- CAUTION: Never use toxic crystals directly in water (Malachite, Azurite, etc.)

### Wearing & Carrying Crystals (p.26–27)
- Wearing keeps crystal's vibration in constant contact with aura
- Right side of body = projective; left = receptive
- Tiara: enhances crown chakra
- Necklaces: work on throat and heart
- Earrings: stiff neck, jaw, head tension
- Rings: little and ring fingers = most chakra-connected

### Crystal Displays (p.30–31)
- Place in home/workspace for continuous energy influence
- Group crystals can work together or neutralize each other — use intuition
- Cleanse regularly (monthly minimum)

### Meditations (p.34–35)
- Lie down, place chakra crystals on body
- Focus on breath, allow crystal energy to flow
- Crystal circle (sitting inside ring of crystals) = deep healing meditation
- 20–30 minutes; ground afterward with smoky quartz or hematite

### The Aura (p.44–47)
- Etheric aura: closest to body; 1–2cm; physical level
- Physical aura: 2–3cm; physical health

- Mental plane aura: up to 25cm; thoughts and mind

## GLOSSARY (p.153–155) — KEY TERMS
- **Chakra:** Sanskrit for "wheel" — spinning energy centers appearing as wheels to energy-sensitive people
- **Chi:** Chinese medicine/philosophy; energy or life force of universe; believed to flow around body and be present in all living things; also called prana (India)
- **Dis-ease:** State of unsoundness on any level (physical, emotional, mental, spiritual); relates to underlying causes, not specific illness
- **Distant healing:** Sending healing energy to person not present; also called absent or remote healing
- **Piezoelectric effect:** Electric current produced by some crystals when subjected to mechanical pressure
- **Piezoelectricity:** Transducer effect that results from applying pressure to crystal; creates physical vibration which in quartz crystal resonates at regular and constant frequency
- **Reiki:** Form of hands-on healing that originated in Japan; now millions of practitioners worldwide
- **Record keeper:** Crystal with raised triangles on face of termination; helps access ancient wisdom and the akashic records
- **Subtle energy:** Energy outside known electromagnetic spectrum; not easily detected
- **Tektite:** Small glassy rock formed from meteorite impact
