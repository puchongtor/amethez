## Principles of Crystal Healing: Formation, Structure and Properties (p. 12)

- Crystals form deep in the earth's crust from superheated gases and mineral-rich solutions at high temperatures finding their way along cracks and fissures
- As they cool, atoms arrange themselves into stable, three-dimensional repeating patterns = crystal lattices
- Harder minerals (diamond, emerald, quartz) form at higher temperature and pressure; softer minerals (calcite, turquoise, selenite) crystallize at lower temperatures
- A crystal may remain unchanged for thousands or millions of years, or go through several transformations
- Example: rhodocrosite can metamorphose from calcite
- The cubic system forms crystals based on cubes — including fluorite, garnet, copper and iron pyrites

## Choosing Crystals (p. 16)

## Cleansing Your Crystals (p. 17)

- New stones need to be cleansed before use; also cleanse after each healing session
- Without cleansing, crystals become less effective and may pass on imbalances or energetic static — felt as heaviness or unpleasantness

### Methods of Cleansing
- **Sun and Water** — Hold stones under running water for a minute then place in sun to dry
- **Incense or Smudge Stick** — Hold crystal within the smoke. Herbs: sage, cedar, sandalwood, frankincense
- **Crystal Cluster** — Place stones on a crystal cluster (e.g. amethyst geode or clear quartz cluster) for 24 hours to clean them; alternatively surround the stone with clear quartz points

- **Visualization** — Take a deep breath and blow over the crystal, imagining you are clearing away negativities. Repeat
- **Sea Salt** — Use dry sea salt, bury the stone for 24 hours. Do NOT use salt water as this damages softer stones

## Quartz Crystals — Types and Shapes (p. 18)

- Quartz can take on many shapes and sizes depending on the conditions prevailing during crystallization
- Different shapes affect the nature of the crystal's energy

### Crystal Shapes and Their Properties
- **Double-Terminated Crystals** — Have a point at each end. Most quartz grows from a rocky base and forms with a single point; if crystallizes in soft mud or clay it may form points at both ends
- **Laser Wands** — Taper towards a very small, faceted point; sides are slightly curved or bent. Ideal for concentrating a tight beam of energy and clearing blocks and stagnant areas
- **Tabular Crystals** — Have two very large flattened faces, as though the crystal has been squashed. This shape increases the speed of energy flowing through it — excellent where extra communication is needed
- **Geodes** — Formed where crystallization occurs in hollow rock chambers originally made by trapped bubbles of air
- **Herkimer Diamonds** — Small, extremely brilliant double-terminated quartz crystals. Wonderful for energizing and cleansing the aura; enhance dreaming and astral travel if placed under the pillow
- **Phantom Crystals** — Within the body of the crystal can be seen smaller outlines of the crystal form (from another mineral that began crystallizing on the surface of the quartz but hasn't inhibited further growth). Make good personal meditation crystals — exemplify the process of growth and "going within"

## The Quartz Family (p. 20)

- Quartz is one of the commonest minerals on earth; greatest number of varieties
- Made up of silicon and oxygen atoms; some varieties form large clear crystals, others are microscopic crystals packed together in massive form
- Coloured varieties occur when a few atoms of another element are included within the lattice structure of the quartz molecules

### Members of the Quartz Family

**Clear Quartz**
- Colourless, water-clear and shiny
- May contain cloudy/milky sections or veils — small bubbles of water or gas trapped within
- Rainbows created by fractures or complex intergrowths that greatly enliven the crystal's energy

**Amethyst**
- Purple or violet in colour
- Chevron amethyst has bands of purple and white
- Excellent general healing stone which calms the mind; good for meditation

**Rose Quartz**
- Pink and translucent; rarely forms large crystals
- Energy is gentle but strong
- Useful balancer of the heart centre and of the emotions

**Citrine Quartz**
- Golden-yellow or orange-brown
- Occurs naturally or is made artificially by heating amethyst
- Energizing stone, physically and mentally

**Smoky Quartz**
- Pale grey-brown to brownish-black
- Excellent grounding stone; acts as a deep cleanser

**Milky Quartz**
- White; contains a high proportion of gas or water bubbles
- Energy is softer and gentler than that of clear quartz

**Rutilated Quartz**
- Contains crystals of rutile — golden or yellow strands of hair or sometimes blades of grass
- Excellent healer of torn or broken tissues

**Tourmaline Quartz**
- Has embedded fine, black needles of tourmaline (occasionally other colours)
- Excellent protecting and strengthening stone

**Aventurine**
- Massive variety of quartz; usually green but sometimes blue
- Identified by tiny spangles of shiny silver mica or golden pyrites throughout
- Green aventurine is a good heart chakra balancer
